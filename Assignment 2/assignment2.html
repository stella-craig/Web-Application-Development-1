Why I Love Stardew Valley

by Stella Craig (smc6fd)

    Fun Facts About Stardew:
  • It was developed by one man: Eric Barone who goes by ConcernedApe.
  • I played over 320 hours before finally 100% a save.
  • It has a co-op mode where you can play with your friends!
  • ConcernedApe taught himself pixel art to make all the art for the game.
  • ConcernedApe also composed all the music for the game.
  • Stardew Valley has Overwhelmingly Positive reviews from over 500,000 reviews on Steam.
Character: About:
Shane is a marriage-able villager in Stardew Valley. He is always the marriage canidate that I marry. I think he has the best character development. He starts off being very rude to the player as he is depressed and an alcoholic, but as you befriend him, he warms up to you and gets help for his mental health. He loves chickens, hot peppers, and pizza. He also will allow the player to purchase blue chickens. His aunt is Marnie and he has a goddaughter named Jas.
Krobus is a monster that lives in the sewers. He is friendly to the player and is the only character that you cannot marry but can still have move into your home as a roommate. He runs a shop full of rare goods, including a sceptor that will return you to your home upon use that costs 2 million gold. Krobus loves diamonds, pumpkins, and void mayonnaise. His name means "bridge-crosser" in his language. On Fridays he never speaks due to the in-game religion. His people just ended an 1,000 year war with the dwarves.

Stardew Valley is one of my favorite games of all time. This is because it is such a pretty game with a beautiful soundtrack. It is such a relazing game as well, as you can take life on your farm as quickly or as slowly as you please. There are many different villagers, all with their own stories and events for you to discover. You have large overarching goals throughout the game, such as completing the Community Center bundles, filling out the museum, and reaching the bottom of the mines. The gameplay is varied enough that you'll never be bored or without something to do. You can get married and have kids, make friends, decorate and build your farm, own farm animals, travel to different lands, and so much more. After quitting your job in the big city and coming back to the farm your grandpa left you, life will never be the same again.
